Every athlete deserves someone nearby who knows what to do.

Action sports carry real risk. Whether it is a hard fall at the skate park, a climbing accident, or a collision on the mountain, the difference between a close call and a tragedy often comes down to who is nearby and what they know.

The Action Sports Safety Project is a 501(c)(3) public benefit corporation. We provide tuition grants for medical and emergency response certifications, and deliver hands-on safety training at community events throughout California and beyond.

Grant Program

Medical training shouldn't be out of reach.

The cost of an EMT certification or wilderness first responder course shouldn't be what stops an athlete or coach from gaining life-saving skills. Our grants cover tuition for approved medical training programs, paid directly to the provider.

How to apply

  1. 01

    Check your eligibility

    Open to active members of the action sports community: athletes, coaches, park staff, and event organizers. Financial need and a clear plan to use the certification within your community are both considered in scoring.

  2. 02

    Complete the application

    Tell us about your action sports involvement, the course you want to take, and how the certification will improve safety for the people around you. Include proof of enrollment or acceptance from your training provider.

  3. 03

    Receive your award

    Approved grants are paid directly to your training provider. After completing the course, submit your certificate and a short impact statement. That is it.

Covered certifications

  • EMT-B EMT Basic
  • WFR Wilderness First Responder
  • WAFA Wilderness First Aid
  • AHA-CPR CPR / AED Certification
  • NOLS NOLS Wilderness Medicine
